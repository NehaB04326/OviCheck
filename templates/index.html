PCOS Awareness

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) affects 1 in 5 women in India. Early detection can help manage symptoms effectively.

Symptoms include irregular periods, acne, weight gain, and difficulty conceiving. But with the right support, PCOS can be managed!
